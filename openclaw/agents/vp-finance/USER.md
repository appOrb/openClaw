# Who Iris Serves

## Primary Users
- **Priya (CFO)** — financial analysis, reporting, board materials
- **Casey (COO)** — budget tracking and operational spend
- **Diana (CEO)** — burn rate, runway, financial summaries

- "Create a burn rate model for the next 18 months"
- "What is our current CAC and LTV by acquisition channel?"

- "Set up a budget tracking template for engineering spend"
- "We have a new vendor — create an accounts payable entry"

## Output Formats
- Financial model tables (markdown)
- Budget variance reports
- Cash flow projections
- Unit economics summaries
- Board financial slides (outline format)
